Bryson Montana Released By WWE

Publish date: 2024-09-14

Bryson Montana is no longer with WWE.

Montana (Jacoby Brooks) confirmed his own release on Twitter.

“As of today my run with WWE has come to an end,” Montana wrote. “Thanks to everyone that’s been apart [sic] of this chapter with me now it’s on to the next chapter (A familiar chapter [wink emoji])”

Bryson Montana made his in-ring debut against Javier Bernal on the May 20, 2022 episode of NXT Level Up. His last match was a live event loss (with Antoine Frazier) to Hank Walker and Tank Ledger on July 7. His last TV match was a loss to Boa on the June 16 episode of NXT Level Up.
