Tinubu Net Worth

Although the exact net worth of Tinubu is unknown, data from multiple sources indicate that the Nigerian billionaire and politician, Tinubu has a staggering net worth of $8.4 billion.

Tinubu made his billions from his career as a politician and from his numerous business investments. Ahmed Tinubu owns the Oando PLC, the largest oil producer in Nigeria. Ahmed Tinubu also owns the Oriental Hotel, a 5-Star hotel situated in Lagos. He also owns other Luxurious hotels across Nigeria.
