Katy Perry Wears aFlamin' Hot Cheeto Costume For Halloween: Photos

Publish date: 2024-08-04

Should this singing thing decline at some point for Katy Perry, she should certainly try her hand at comedy. The "Roar" singer, 30, said no to the typical sexy costume in favor of a LOL-worthy Flamin' Hot Cheeto getup for Kate Hudson's annual Halloween bash on Thursday, Oct. 30, in Los Angeles.

 

Perry, who just celebrated her birthday on Oct. 25, sported a large orange suit over red leggings, and a bright red lip. The star accessorized with a Cheetos handbag and matching sneakers.

 

Perry's choice for the fave snack food may seem random — but it's not. The "Dark Horse" singer once relied on Cheetos as comfort food following her divorce from ex-husband Russell Brand in 2011.
