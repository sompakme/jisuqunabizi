She Didn't Leave Jason Sudeikis For Harry Styles

Publish date: 2024-07-31

Olivia Wilde has had quite a year in the tabloids both professionally and personally. She's taking the time to finally respond to the rumors that she cheated on her ex-fiance Jason Sudeikis with pop star actor Harry Styles. Well, apparently she didn't and wants fans to know. As press for Wilde's next film Don't Worry Darling reached dramatic proportions, Wilde's personal life and relationship history was highly scrutinized as well.

Olivia had previously been suspected of cheating on Jason with Harry, and she is setting the record straight. Olivia and Jason ended a 7-year engagement during the pandemic and share two young children.

Their split was made public in November 2020 and Wilde was seen out with Styles soon after sparking affair rumors and questions about the timelines of the two relationships.

Many didn't seem to take into consideration that it was very possible that Olivia and Jason had split some months before they made the official announcement. But that's exactly what according to Wilde herself.

Olivia and Jason Met After Saturday Night Live

The famous exes first met at a Saturday Night Live after-party. Every Saturday night after a live show, the cast and crew hold an after-party to celebrate the show. These parties can get wild. They're also places of romance.

Wilde attended an SNL after-party in the spring of 2011 where she first met the comedian, but they didn't start dating until a few months later. They hit it off and eventually got engaged before welcoming their first child together in 2014.

They had a second child in 2016 but remained unmarried. As the long engagement continued, there were questions about if marriage was going to actually happen.

Now fans know that marriage wasn't in the cards for them. The details aren't well known, but their relationship afterward hasn't been positive.

There have been a few instances of conflict between the ex-partners.

The Odd Timing Of Harry Styles Plus Olivia Wilde

Olive Wilde has been in the movie industry for a while and was making her second movie as a director when she met Harry Styles. The now much-talked-about Don't Worry Darling had a major casting issue and Harry Styles replaced Shia Labeouf in the middle of the shoot, but that's a whole different story.

Wilde began dating the actor she hired soon after filming began in late 2020. Notably, Olivia was Harry's boss who had hired him to do a job. This is a situation that may be questionable all on its own.

The two were spotted holding hands in January 2021 after there were rumors of their on-set romance. While this did raise professional eyebrows, curious ones were also very interested in the relationship.

It appeared to fans that Olivia moved on very quickly from her longtime beau and the father of her two kids. However, there may be more to the story that the public didn't really know which isn't surprising.

Olivia and Jason split during the pandemic months before she started dating Styles.

Olivia Admits That She Left Jason

After the whirlwind of news that Wilde was dating Harry Styles, there was another event that threatened to overshadow a press conference for her second film. Wilde was served with court papers as soon as she stepped on the stage at a press.

The papers were revealed to be custody related to their children. Needless to say, Wilde wasn't happy about this. ​​

This didn't give Jason the best press, but he did end up publicly apologizing claiming he had no role in where his ex was served. Olivia stated in a Vanity Fair profile that while many people were shocked and surprised that she was served on stage, she wasn't and believes it was done intentionally. She even said, "Unfortunately, I wasn’t that shocked. There’s a reason that I didn’t stay in that relationship. Unfortunately, that was consistent with my experience of the relationship."

Wilde went on to comment on all the criticism she receives for being a mom who also lives her own life. This past year didn't seem like it was an easy one for Wilde, but she confronted these rumors head-on.

According to the actress herself, she and Sudeikis were done before she even met Styles. This relationship attracts a lot of attention as well, and fans are wondering how long Styles and Wilde will last.
