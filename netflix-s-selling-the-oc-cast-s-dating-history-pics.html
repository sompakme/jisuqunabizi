Netflixs Selling the OC Casts Dating History: Pics

Publish date: 2024-08-09

The cast of Selling the OC turned heads for their complicated — and very flirty — office relationships during season 1, and the drama is still going strong.

Tyler Stanaland’s intimate friendships with Alexandra “Alex” Hall and Polly Brindle, for example, raised some eyebrows when the show premiered in August 2022, as Tyler was married to Brittany Snow during filming. The trio turned heads when they gave each other “nosies,” a.k.a. open mouth kisses.

Tyler denied that his flirty relationships with both women were inappropriate, but he did confess that Kayla Cardona‘s advances were not OK. Kayla, for her part, exclusively told Us Weekly in August 2022 she was “feeling the reciprocation back” when she tried to kiss Tyler.

“It really felt like brother, sister vibes in most of our opinions,” Tyler argued to Us at the time, claiming he didn’t lead Kayla on. “It never felt that way, but I can’t speak for her.”
