Eva Longoria Explains Red Bracelet From Honeymoon: Red Carpet Photos

Publish date: 2024-07-24

Wearing her heart on her wrist! Eva Longoria hit the red carpet premiere of Devious Maids on Thursday, June 2, in Los Angeles, wearing a stunning black dress and one very special bracelet. And you can learn all about the special accessory in Red Carpet Daily, presented by AT&T.

Just weeks after marrying Jose “Pepe” Baston in Mexico, the actress, 41, sported a scarlet string tied to one wrist, which came straight from her honeymoon in Cambodia. “[It was a] wedding blessing from a monk,” Longoria told Us Weekly on the red carpet. “He tied this on my hand and one on Pepe’s, and then he blessed us with jasmine flowers.”
