Jane Seymour Poses for Playboy at 67: Pic!

Publish date: 2024-08-07

She’s still strutting her stuff! Jane Seymour is proving that age is just a number after modeling for Playboy magazine at age 67.

The James Bond actress took to Instagram on Wednesday, February 21, to debut an image from the stunning shoot. “I’m THRILLED to finally share this with you! I was recently photographed and interviewed in my home by Playboy,” Seymour, dressed in a fuzzy pink sweater and sheer lingerie, wrote. “I open up about my career, my family, feeling better-than-ever at 67 and so much more!”

While speaking to the publication, Seymour opened up about feeling more confident and sexy at this stage of her life than she ever has before. “I feel much sexier now than I ever did when I was younger. Then I was like, ‘Oh gosh, I’m supposed to be sexy. What is that?! There’s an enormous freedom in having lived as long as I have,” she explained. “Like my father used to say, I’m comfortable in my own skin.”

In fact, the Emmy winner revealed that she often gets hit on by men half her age! “I said, ‘Oh darn, in another life.’ I find that very flattering,” she admitted. “I mean, usually you’re invisible by this age! Your natural instinct is to close up your heart and let it eat you up. Do something to help someone. It will heal you. You’ll be like a magnet when you do that. Light to firefly.”
