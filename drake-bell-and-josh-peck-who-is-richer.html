Drake Bell And Josh Peck: Who Is Richer?

Publish date: 2024-09-08

In 2004, Drake Bell and Josh Peck had become household names thanks to their successful stint on the hit Nickelodeon series, Drake & Josh, spanning four seasons, 57 episodes, and a critically-acclaimed feature-length TV movie titled Drake & Josh Go Hollywood.

Their triumphant run on Nickelodeon opened the doors for both of its stars to carry on with very successful careers in Hollywood as Bell went on to sign his first record deal with Nine Yards Records while Peck carried on acting both on the small and big screen.

After making appearances in several of his friend David Dobrik’s YouTube videos in 2016, the 33-year-old decided to launch his own channel, which has since amassed a whopping 3.7 million subscribers and subsequently earning Peck a fortune in revenue. But who has built a higher net worth: Drake or Josh?

Updated on September 3, 2021, by Michael Chaar: Nickelodeon's Drake & Josh was easily one of the biggest shows. While the series paid both actors quite well, their current net worths are nowhere near comparable. Josh Peck takes the win with a whopping net worth of $9 million, which he amassed through the success of his YouTube and social media presence. As for Drake Bell, he comes in with a measly net worth of $600,000. Bell encountered many financial woes throughout his career, including filing for bankruptcy in 2014. Things only went downhill for Drake following his arrest this past summer due to child endangerment. Following his July 12, 2021 hearing, Drake Bell has been sentenced to 2 years probation.

Who Is Richer: Drake Bell Or Josh Peck?

It comes to nobody's surprise that Josh Peck is the richer star out of the two, having made most of his recent income from his successful YouTube career.

The New York native has accumulated an incredible 345 million views since making his debut on the video-sharing platform back in 2017, with an estimated income upwards of $420,000 a year, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

Of course, this doesn’t include any sponsored deals and partnerships Peck may sign to promote and endorse products in his videos, which can easily earn him another $50,000 for a simple shoutout to his millions of subscribers. Peck’s YouTube videos currently average anywhere between 200k-300k while his videos such as his reunion with former co-star Miranda Cosgrove, along with his wedding special, having piled up over five million views.

The Mean Creek star also has 11.5 million followers on Instagram, and if you know a thing or two about sponsorships, you’d be well aware of the fact that the more people that are following a specific account, the more money they can demand from companies who are interested to promote their products on their page.

It’s believed that Peck’s net worth has skyrocketed to $9 million thanks to YouTube and Instagram while his acting career is still going strong with recent projects including the TV series including Teenage Mutant Ninja Turtles and Grandfathered.

Drake And Josh's Falling Out

Bell, on the other hand, hasn’t been too lucky with his income, which is surprising since he would probably be just as successful as Peck if he was to dedicate his time to creating a YouTube channel of his own. To date, the 35-year-old has released four albums, which didn't go on to do too well, so much that in February 2014, he was forced to file for bankruptcy after ending up with over $581,000 in debt, according to TMZ.

Drake went on to experience major financial woes, which left him with a net worth of $600,000, which obviously isn’t anywhere near the numbers Peck has amassed, but at least he’s no longer bankrupt. His money troubles weren't the only thing going awry for Drake. The star was left out of Josh's wedding party, leading to an online rant that led the two to fall out.

Luckily for the two, they reunited on Peck's YouTube channel back in 2017, squashing the beef the two had, especially when it came to Drake not receiving an invite to Josh's nuptials. Although things were finally getting back to normal for Bell, he found himself having problems with the law!

Drake Bell Arrested

As if things weren't bad enough for Drake Bell, the former Nickelodeon star was arrested on child endangerment charges earlier this summer. In June 2021, the actor and singer pleaded guilty via Zoom to felony charges of "attempting to endanger children and a misdemeanor charge of disseminating material harmful to juveniles," says Deadline.

The sentencing occurred back on July 12, where Drake was sentenced to 2 years probation, ultimately avoiding any prison time. All of this came as quite a shock to fans, who idolized the star growing up.
