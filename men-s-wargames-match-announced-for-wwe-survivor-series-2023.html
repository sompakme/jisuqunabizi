Mens WarGames Match Announced For WWE Survivor Series 2023

Publish date: 2024-07-18

Cody Rhodes will go to war at WWE Survivor Series.

In the closing moments of the November 6 episode of WWE RAW, Cody Rhodes, Jey Uso, Sami Zayn, and Seth Rollins brawled with The Judgment Day (Finn Balor, Damian Priest, and Dominik Mysterio) and JD McDonagh. Adam Pearce came out, determined to reign in the chaos.

But he only amped everyone up when he announced that the two groups would clash in a WarGames match at WWE Survivor Series on November 25.

WWE Chief Content Officer Triple H then shared a new poster for WWE Survivor Series: WarGames, which shows Cody Rhodes preparing for war.

The two teams for the WarGames bouts are currently incomplete, as the contest typically features five-man lineups. Fans will have to wait and see how the two teams come together.

The aforementioned brawl came after Rollins beat Zayn to retain the WWE World Heavyweight Championship on Monday.
