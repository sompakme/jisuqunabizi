Sabrina Carpenter's Dating History: Joshua Bassett, More

Publish date: 2024-07-15

Sabrina Carpenter has remained coy about her dating history — but that doesn’t mean she’s avoided romantic drama.

The Girl Meets World alum’s love life first made headlines in August 2020 when she was spotted out to lunch in Los Angeles with Joshua Bassett. The pair’s rumored romance raised eyebrows as many fans thought Bassett had been dating Olivia Rodrigo in secret.

Rodrigo fueled the drama in January 2021 after releasing her debut single, “Driver’s License,” that referenced a “blonde” who was “so much older than me.” Carpenter, for her part, denied recording her own “diss track” in January 2021 after the release of her song “Skin.” In 2022, Carpenter’s song “Because I Liked a Boy” caused fans to once again think there was a love triangle between her, Rodrigo and Bassett.

Bassett confirmed in December 2021 that he was single, and Carpenter has since been linked to Shawn Mendes, among others.
