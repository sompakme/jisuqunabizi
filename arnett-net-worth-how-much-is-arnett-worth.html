Will Arnett, born on May 4, 1970, in Toronto, Canada, embarked on his journey in the entertainment industry with a passion for voice acting. Starting his career behind the microphone, he lent his unique vocal talents to various projects, including commercials and animated series.

As he honed his craft, Arnett transitioned to on-screen acting, marking a new chapter in his career.
