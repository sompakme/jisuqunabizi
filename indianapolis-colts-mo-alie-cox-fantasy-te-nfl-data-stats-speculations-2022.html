Player Outlook

Mo Alie-Cox, a 6-foot-5, 267-pound tight end for the Indianapolis Colts, re-signed with the organization in March on a three-year deal. With Jack Doyle’s retirement, Mo should play a more significant role in 2022.

Mo Alie-Cox’s path to the NFL was unlikely. The Indianapolis Colts made the decision to prioritize Mo Alie-Cox as a rookie free agency following the NFL draft as a result of his strong workout. Since then, the Colts have spent the last four seasons molding the physically freakishly uber-athletic player.

Mo has demonstrated several times that he can play forcefully with the ball in his hands due to his outstanding size and strength. You can see him using his power in the example below to drive past the defense and secure the first down.
