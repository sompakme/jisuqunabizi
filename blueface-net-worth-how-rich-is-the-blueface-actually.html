Family and Early Life

Blueface was born on 20th January 1997 in Los Angeles, California. He grew up in Mid-City in Los Angeles. Blueface attended several elementary schools and later moved in with his mother in Santa Clarita Valley before settling down with his father in Oakland.

He attended Arlete High School where he joined the marching band playing alto saxophone and also took part in the school’s football team. He proceeded to Fayetteville State University where played in the college football team.

Blueface was in a relationship with two women before recently breaking up with one and has been in a relationship with his current girlfriend whom they have a son named Javaughn.
