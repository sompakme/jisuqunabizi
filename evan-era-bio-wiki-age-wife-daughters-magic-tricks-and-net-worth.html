Career

Evan is a magician and YouTube prankster with a substantial online presence, boasting over 3.1 million subscribers on his EvanEraTV YouTube channel. He is renowned for sharing magic trick tutorials, social experiments, and pranks. In addition to his main channel, he runs a second channel dedicated to vlogging titled EvanEraVlogs. Interestingly, instead of initiating his channel with a prank, Evan introduced it with a video titled “Evan Era vs Jus Juice Rap Battle.”

One of Evan’s standout YouTube videos, “3 EASY Magic Tricks – How To Magic!,” has garnered considerable attention, accumulating over 1 million views since its posting in February 2015. His content showcases a mix of magical demonstrations and entertaining pranks, contributing to his popularity in the online community.
