What Happened To The 'Married At First Sight' Season 10 Couples?

Publish date: 2024-07-17

Married at First Sight is a reality TV show that first aired in 2014. Now in its 14th season, the series brings together couples who claim to be ready for marriage through experts and specialists. The catch is that the participants won’t meet their partner until the bride walks down the aisle to say her vows with her husband-to-be.

Season 10 aired in 2020, spotlighting five marriages. It was quite a wild ride that surfaced lies, emotional explosions, and many tears. Two years later, fans of the show are wondering what has happened to these hopeful couples, and we have the answers for you.

9 Jessica & Austin Hurd Are Now Parents

Jessica Studer and Austin Hurd are the only matched couple that have remained married. While their marriage undoubtedly had some hardships to overcome, they have put in the work to make their relationship as healthy as possible, allowing them to thrive as husband and wife. They currently have a son who was born in the beginning of November, and they are loving family life.

8 Brandon Reid Holds Disdain Towards The Show

Brandon Reid was paired with Taylor Dunklin, and while on the surface they seemed like an evenly matched couple, the honeymoon brought about intense arguments, leading to Brandon cursing at the Married at First Sight production team. He has a restraining order against Taylor and has stayed off social media since his emotional explosion. Reid moved for a marriage annulment and continues to keep his life private.

7 Taylor Dunklin Is Working As A Researcher At Johns Hopkins University

Taylor Dunklin, who was matched with Brandon Reid, has recently made her life more private. After the tumultuous events that occurred on her honeymoon with Brandon, she filed a restraining order against Brandon so that neither could be in the same place at the same time going forward. What we do know about Dunklin, however, is that she continues to keep up with the other wives from season 10 while she works as a researcher for Johns Hopkins University.

6 Meka Jones Is Trying To Live Her Best Life

Meka Jones was the youngest of the participants for season 10, joining at only 25 years old. Throughout her marriage, she discovered that her husband Michael Wilson was constantly feeding her lies, which led to distrust between the couple. She ended up wanting a divorce and has since been living it up the best she can with her girlfriends, traveling and celebrating life.

5 Michael Wilson Is Staying Off The Grid

Following the confrontation of Meka sharing she that had trouble believing him, Michael in turn felt he could not fully open up emotionally to his wife. Michael Wilson grieved a familial loss while the show was recording and claims that Meka did not comfort him enough. Between both individuals’ struggles, they ended up splitting and Wilson has since stayed off social media and out of the news.

4 Derek Sherman Is Single And Traveling

Derek Sherman was matched with Katie Conrad, and while they had several hiccups during the show, ended up agreeing to stay together and signing a year-long lease with each other. Unfortunately, the two ended up deciding they just didn’t belong together and split. Derek has since been traveling the world, visiting mountain ranges, forests, oceans, and everything in between. He has been enjoying his favorite hobbies as a single man, embracing every moment as it comes.

3 Katie Conrad Is Now Married Thanks To Hinge

Katie Conrad was the logical realist of the couple, constantly trying to bring Derek the dreamer back down to her level. After their split, Conrad went private on social media, keeping her personal life away from prying eyes. While we don’t have too much information on the new couple, we can share that she is now married to a man named Brandon Eaves, whom she met on the Hinge dating app.

2 Zach Justice Is Focusing On Health And Fitness

Zach Justice was a somewhat controversial husband on Married at First Sight, sharing that he lacked a physical and emotional connection to his wife, going so far as to flirt with her friend while they were out. After deciding to divorce, Justice took some time for personal growth and focused his passions on health and fitness. He is now a trainer and the founder of Casual Athlete.

1 Mindy Shiben Is Living A Single And Fulfilling Life

Following Zach’s lack of commitment and his relational betrayal, Mindy Shiben called off the relationship and stood up for herself. From her public posts, it seems she is currently single, but that doesn’t stop her from following her heart. Mindy has been adventuring around the country and also enjoys fitness, recently entering and completing a Tough Mudder run.
