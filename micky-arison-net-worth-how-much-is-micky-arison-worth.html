Early Life and Family

Micky Arison, born in Tel Aviv, Israel in 1949, had a unique introduction to the cruise industry through his father, Ted Arison. Ted, along with Knut Kloster, co-founded Norwegian Caribbean Lines, a pioneering cruise ship company that revolutionized the industry. Micky’s early life was intertwined with the world of cruising, as his father’s business venture paved the way for his own future success.
