Over the last several years, Owen Wilson has suddenly found himself in the spotlight once again. Thanks to him reuniting with Jennifer Lopez in a movie and starring in the Marvel Cinematic Universe series Loki, many fans remembered what they liked so much about Wilson.

Now that fans have been reminded about Wilson, many of them have been interested to learn more about his personal life. A perfect example of that is there is an increased interest in Wilson's relationship with his children.

As it turns out, many fans who've learned more about Wilson's kids have been shocked by how differently he reportedly treats his children.

What Is Owen Wilson's Relationship With His Son Robert Like?

In the years since Owen Wilson became a star, the popular actor has found himself in a harsh spotlight on more than one occasion. In some cases, that has been for weird reasons like the time Wilson's brother Luke Wilson lied about him and got banned from the Playboy mansion.

On the other hand, there is no doubt that Wilson's love life has made gossip websites and the tabloids pay attention to him in the past. After all, Wilson has dated other stars like Kate Hudson, Gina Gershon, and Sheryl Crow, and he was rumored to have been involved with Demi Moore.

When Wilson hasn't been dating celebrities, he has gotten involved with three anonymous women he had children with. First off, Wilson welcomed his first child into the world when he was dating Jade Duell in 2011.

Given the name Robert Ford Wilson, Wilson's oldest son is twelve as of this writing, and he reportedly goes by his middle name. From all accounts, Wilson has a close relationship with his eldest son and the pair has been captured on film together many times in the past.

"It seems surreal that this tiny human being is now here — and I'm thinking about all the great adventures he has ahead of him." During a separate interview on The Tonight Show, Wilson called Ford Wilson "a little scene stealer". As Ford Wilson started to grow up a little, Wilson spoke to Ellen DeGeneres about his son who was 6 years old at the time. According to Wilson, his eldest son seemed like he might have a future in the entertainment industry at that time.

"Ford seems like he might even be a little stuntman in training." Wilson continued, "He's a big Jackie Chan fan." Wilson then went on to describe Ford Wilson's sense of humor. "Anything Ford says is just the funniest thing ever. And I can make a joke and it's, like, crickets."

Does Owen Wilson See His Daughter Lyla?

Following Lyla Wilson's birth, it was revealed to the public that Wilson wanted nothing to do with his daughter. Following that revelation, many people found that news to be so surprising that they questioned if Wilson's fans would abandon the actor.

While Wilson's career is going strong today, some observers have been fascinated by how differently Wilson treats his daughter from his two sons. In 2019, the mother of Wilson's daughter spoke to The Daily Mail and revealed that Lyla Wilson had zero contact with her father. "Owen has never met Lyla. Never."

Vongsvirates then went on to talk about what she believes Lyla Wilson needs most from the movie star. "Lyla needs a father. It’s ironic how [Owen] keeps getting these father roles, he's playing a father in his new movie, and he's never met his own daughter."

Since Wilson has never spoken about his daughter publicly, his side of the story has never been revealed. Most notably, there is no way to know why he embraces being a father to his sons while having nothing to do with his daughter aside from sending money.
