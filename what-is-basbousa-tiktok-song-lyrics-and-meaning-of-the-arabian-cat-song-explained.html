What Is The Basbousa TikTok Song?

The Basbousa TikTok song is an Arabic cat song that is currently being adored by thousands of people all over the world.

According to reports, the song is a remake of a well-known northern Arabic music from either Syria or Lebanon. The majority of people believe the song was written in Lebanon.

The magnificent voice and the catchy tempo are what makes the track so captivating that it is adored by individuals who don’t even speak the language.

Basbousa Song Or Arabic Cat Love Song Lyrics Explored 

The lyrics of Basbousa, also known as the Arabic cat love song, tells the story of two cats.

The song is about a romantic relationship between a male and a female cat. The song also explains how the male cat refers to the female cat as “basbousa,” which is Arabic for “desert.”

Similarly, the song continues by saying that the male cat follows his lover from one house to another and asks her to give him a kiss.

However, as the official words of the song have yet to be revealed, the aforementioned lyrics and description of the song are only a description supplied by many middle eastern folks in the comments area of YouTube and various other sites.
