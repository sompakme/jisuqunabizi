Since most people love movies and TV shows, it may seem like the average fan would have a pretty good idea of how making money as an actor works. In reality, however, most people have a lot of misperceptions about how famous actors cash in.

One of the biggest misconceptions that a lot of people seem to have about how Hollywood works is they believe an actor's salary is directly connected to how famous they are. While, that certainly plays a role in how much money an actor gets paid, other factors are much more important.

In reality, movie studios and television networks don't really care that much about how famous an actor is. Instead, they are interested in how much money an actor's past movies have brought in at the box office and how well an actor's past shows performed in the ratings.
