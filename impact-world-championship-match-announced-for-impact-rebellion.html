IMPACT World Championship Match Announced For IMPACT Rebellion

Publish date: 2024-09-13

The potential main event of has been announced.

IMPACT Wrestling has confirmed that Josh Alexander will defend the IMPACT World Championship against Steve Maclin at the show on April 16. Maclin earned the title shot by winning a four-way match at IMPACT No Surrender on February 24. Later in the show, Alexander retained his title against former world champion Rich Swann, and IMPACT subsequently conformed that Maclin will get his shot at Rebellion.

BREAKING: @Walking_Weapon will defend the IMPACT World Championship against @SteveMaclin at #Rebellion on April 16 from the Rebel Entertainment Complex in Toronto, Canada.
