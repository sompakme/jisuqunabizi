Norman Reedus News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-07-27

Name:Norman Reedus

Hometown:Hollywood, Florida

Birthday:January 6, 1969

About

Born in Hollywood, Florida, to parents Marianne and Ira, Norman Reedus is best known for his role as Daryl Dixon in the AMC series The Walking Dead and his appearance in the 1999 film The Boondocks Saints and its 2009 sequel.

In addition to acting, the Florida native has modeled for various brands including Prada, Levi’s and Lexus. He also provided motion capture and voice acting for the character Sam Porter in the 2019 video game Death Stranding.

Reedus met Diane Kruger while filming Sky in 2015 and the pair made their red carpet debut in 2018. That November, they welcomed a daughter, Nova. The actor was previously married to Danish model and photographer Helena Christensen from 1998 to 2003 and shares son Mingus, born in 1999, with her.
