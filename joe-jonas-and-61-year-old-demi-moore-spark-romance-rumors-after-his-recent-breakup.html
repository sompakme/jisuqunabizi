Joe Jonas And 61-Year-Old Demi Moore Spark Romance Rumors After His Recent Breakup

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Highlights

  • Joe Jonas and Demi Moore have sparked rumors of a romance after being seen getting close at the Cannes Film Festival.
  • Sources have confirmed that Joe and Demi have bonded and spent time together, but deny there's any romantic relationship yet.
  • Joe Jonas recently ended his relationship with model Stormi Bree and is focusing on his kids and career for now.

The most unlikely celebrity couple may have formed at the Cannes Film Festival. Joe Jonas, who recently broke up with model Stormi Bree and is still dealing with his divorce from Sophie Turner, is apparently getting close to a legendary actress almost 30 years older than him.

Demi Moore allegedly met the Jonas Brothers singer at this year's Cannes Film Festival and they clicked immediately. Fans have been speculating on their relationship ever since they were spotted on what looked very much like a date in France, and recent reports have confirmed that the two of them have "bonded" ever since they met.

Demi Moore And Joe Jonas Have Been Bonding

Newly single singer Joe Jonas and Demi Moore were spotted out together in France, having lunch and acting very flirty with each other, which raised questions about the nature of their relationship.

Amid the rumors and speculation, a source spoke to Page Six, hinting that something was growing between them and they were getting closer.

"Demi and Joe have struck up a friendship. They have mutual friends and they bonded," the source shared. The pair allegedly met at the Cannes Film Festival, and the connection was immediate. The age difference (Demi Moore is 61 and Joe Jonas is 34) and the short time they have known each other has confused fans, but according to the source, they are still getting to know each other.

Another source has weighed in, denying that there's a romance between Demi and Joe, but they haven't denied that they are spending time together.

"Joe and Demi are just friends," the insider told Us Weekly. "There’s nothing romantic going on between them." Another source had told the publication not long ago that the singer was not looking to date anyone right now.

"Joe has a very busy schedule between his kids and his career, so his romantic life has to take a back seat for now," the source stated.

Joe Jonas Recently Broke Up With Ex-Girlfriend Stormi Bree

A couple of days ago, it was announced that Joe Jonas and model Stormi Bree had broken up after just a few months of dating.

"Joe Jonas and Stormi Bree are no longer together," a source shared with Entertainment Tonight on June 2nd. "It has been a little while since they broke up." The rumors of a romance heating up with Demi Moore started shortly after.

Stormi Bree was Joe Jonas' first girlfriend (that the world knows of) since his divorce from Sophie Turner, and while it never turned into anything serious, the singer was reportedly very happy with her.

"Joe is enjoying spending time with Stormi," a source said at the time. "His main priority and focus will always be his children, but at the same time, he’s putting himself out there and is open to what’s to come. Things are still very new, but he’s happy and he’s open to seeing where things go."

As things stand, it seems Joe Jonas is happy to stay single for a while and focus on parenthood and his music career.
