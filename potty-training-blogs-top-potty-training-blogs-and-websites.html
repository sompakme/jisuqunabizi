Potty Training Best PracticesPotty Training Tips
  • Encourage positive reinforcement and rewards
  • Create a consistent and structured routine
  • Use child-sized potties or potty seat inserts
  • Introduce the concept of using the toilet gradually
  • Keep a potty training chart to track progress
  • Offer plenty of fluids to encourage regular bathroom visits
