Donny Pangilinan is Belle Mariano Boyfriend material. The Filipino actor Donny also admires her. But is the on-screen couple dating or not? Let’s find it out!

Belinda Angelito Mariano, shortly known as Belle Mariano, is a beautiful actress, singer, and model. With her never-ending allure, charm, and epic performance, she has won the hearts of millions.

She is the first-ever Filipina recipient of the Outstanding Asian Star award at the Seoul International Drama Awards.

She began appearing on TV at nine and finally landed her lead role in a 2020 Filipino family comedy-drama film Four Sisters Before the Wedding. Let’s learn more about the star’s love life in detail.

Is Donny Pangilinan Belle Mariano Boyfriend?

Belle Mariano’s on-screen partner Donny Pangilinan spoke about their relationship. During an interview with presenter Karen Davila via her YouTube channel on 22 January 2022, the actor said they are in a beautiful place.

He also expressed gratitude toward their situation and said he and Belle are blessed to work together. Although the answer was ambiguous and indirect, it was meaningful.

In the same interview, the actor admired Belle as a very focused woman. Donny said she separates herself and goes with what she thinks without caring what other people will say. That is what He’s Into Her star likes about his co-star.

Donny Pangilinan is Belle Mariano’s boyfriend material. The actress admitted that the fellow actor is her type. She went on to say they are focused on work and happy with one another’s company. Belle likes the supportive nature of her on-screen partner.

Belle and Donny crossed paths with their shared career. They were an on-screen couple in the drama He’s Into Her. After fan witnessed their incredible chemistry in the TV series, their dating started to surface. The talented actor and actress also worked as a couple in Love Is Color Blind.
